   - **Damages Formula:** (Fair Rental Value if Warranted) - (Fair Rental Value Unfit)
   - **Cap:** Cannot exceed total rent paid
   - **Your Case:** ($1,781 - $890) × 21 months = $18,711

2. **N.C.G.S. § 42-42 Habitability Warranty**
   - **Landlord Duty:** "Make all repairs...put and keep premises fit and habitable"
   - **Written Notice:** Required for (a)(4) repairs, subject to exception
   - **Portal Requests:** MAA portal = written notice (timestamped, documented)

   - **Holding:** Tenants entitled to rent abatement for period premises unfit
   - **Application:** 7+ months of mold/HVAC issues → rent abatement justified

   - **Holding:** Tenant's written waiver of RRAA rights has no legal effect
