## MAA Comparison (Lessons Learned)

### ✅ What 110 Frazier MUST Have (Non-Negotiable)
Based on MAA failures:

1. **Clear repair timeline**: "Landlord shall complete non-emergency repairs within 7 days"
2. **Rent abatement clause**: "Tenant may withhold rent if landlord fails to maintain habitability"
3. **Work order tracking**: Online portal or written confirmation of all repair requests
4. **Emergency contact**: 24/7 phone number with <24 hour response guarantee
5. **Photo documentation**: Tenant has right to document all issues without landlord interference

---

### ❌ What 110 Frazier MUST NOT Have (Dealbreakers)
Based on MAA red flags:

1. **No "AS-IS" clause**: Property must be habitable on move-in
2. **No blanket liability waiver**: Landlord cannot disclaim all maintenance duties
3. **No rent withholding prohibition**: Tenant must have leverage for repairs
4. **No excessive penalties**: Late fees >$50 or lease breakage >2 months' rent
5. **No forced arbitration**: Tenant must retain right to sue in court
