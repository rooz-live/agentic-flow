## Legal Arguments (Demand Letter Strategy)

### 1. Constructive Eviction (Primary Argument)
**Elements** (all must be satisfied):
1. ✅ Landlord breached duty to maintain premises
2. ✅ Breach substantially interfered with use/enjoyment
3. ✅ Tenant gave notice + opportunity to repair
4. ✅ Landlord failed to remedy within reasonable time
5. ⚠️ Tenant vacated premises (or intends to vacate)

**Amanda's case**: If she has documented MAA failures (work orders, photos), she satisfies all elements.
