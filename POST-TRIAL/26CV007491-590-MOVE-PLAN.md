**Why is filing an appeal a priority if I'm moving?**  
The appeal deadline (March 20) is **fixed by rule**. If you don't file a Notice of Appeal by then, you lose the option to appeal this judgment. Moving doesn't change that. So L1 is high WSJF because it's **time-bound**: decide and (if you want the option) file by 3/20. A Motion to Stay Execution (L2) can then ask the court to pause eviction/removal while the appeal is pending—so appeal + stay together support both preserving your legal position and buying time for an orderly move. If you skip appeal, you're accepting the judgment as final and focusing only on move logistics.
