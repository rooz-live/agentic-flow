Circle Lead, Innovation (Lead Link):
- Prioritizes the Innovation Circle’s strategy and assigns roles
- Not a boss
- An integrator of accountabilities

Anti-patterns to avoid
- Centralizing all approvals in the Circle Lead—put gates on specific domains (e.g., ethics checklists, model eval suite).
- No kill criteria—creates zombie projects; define thresholds up front.
- Demos without adoption—require adoption metrics and a handoff plan as part of “Definition of Done” for ventures.
- One mega “Innovation” role—split by domain (discovery, prototyping, accounting, scouting).
