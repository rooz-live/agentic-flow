# Circle Lead, Accountabilities (“Orchestrator” Lead Link)

## Primary Mandate
To ensure the seamless flow of value delivery by managing dependencies, cadence, and integration across all circles.

## Accountabilities
- Keep flow
- Dependencies
- Cadence
- Integration healthy

## Metrics
- **Lead Time**: Concept to Cash (Target: < 5 days)
- **Cycle Time**: Start to Finish (Target: < 2 days)
- **Throughput**: Items per Sprint (Target: > 20)
- **WIP Violations**: Number of times work-in-progress limits are exceeded (Target: 0)

**Key Metrics:**
*   **Flow Metrics:**
    *   Lead Time
    *   Cycle Time
    *   Throughput
    *   WIP Violations
