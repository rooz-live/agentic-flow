## STATUTORY VIOLATION

Under **N.C.G.S. § 42-42**, landlords must maintain heating systems in good working order. Indoor temperatures below 65°F during heating season constitute a habitability violation.

**Landlord Failed to Provide**:
- Functional heating system from Jan 1 - Feb 10 (40+ days)
- Reasonable response to maintenance requests (14+ days average)
- Safe and habitable living conditions
