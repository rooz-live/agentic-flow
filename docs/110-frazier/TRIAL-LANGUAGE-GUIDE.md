**Date:** 2026-03-01  
**Trial Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Case:** Artchat v MAA (26CV005596-590)

---

## 🎯 Core Principle: Truth & Accuracy

**DO NOT claim income you don't have.** This guide helps you frame **future earning capacity** (which you CAN prove) vs **current income** (which you CANNOT prove yet).

---

## ❌ AVOID: False Income Claims

### What NOT to Say
1. ❌ "I earned $X from trading" (paper trading = $0 real income)
2. ❌ "I have steady income from consulting" (no signed contracts = no income)
3. ❌ "My bank account shows $X deposits" (unless true with bank statements)
4. ❌ "I'm currently employed at [company]" (if employment is blocked)
5. ❌ "I generate $X/month from my business" (if no realized revenue)

### Why These Are Dangerous
- **Perjury risk:** False statements under oath
- **Credibility loss:** Judge will distrust ALL your testimony
- **Sanctions:** Potential Rule 11 sanctions for frivolous claims
- **Landlord ammunition:** "Plaintiff admitted false income claims"

## 🎭 Trial Dialogue Examples

### If Judge Asks: "What is your current income?"

**❌ BAD ANSWER:** "I make $900/month from trading."  
**Reason:** False (paper trading = $0 real income)

**✅ GOOD ANSWER:**  
"Your Honor, I don't currently have traditional employment income due to the employment blocking situation (Case #3, Apex). However, I have **demonstrable earning capacity** through:
1. Operational software systems (neural trader, built Feb 28)
2. Consulting agreements in pipeline ($75/hr, 10 hours = $750)
3. Agentics coaching expertise (Lean Agile + Data Analytics)

I'm prepared to provide documentation of these capabilities, including signed consulting contracts (as of March 2) and operational system evidence."

---

### If Judge Asks: "Can you afford $3,400/month rent?"

**❌ BAD ANSWER:** "Yes, I have income from my business."  
**Reason:** Vague, no evidence, easily challenged

**✅ GOOD ANSWER:**  
"Your Honor, at the time I signed the lease (Feb 27), I was under housing crisis pressure (duress). I now have:
1. **Immediate earning capacity:** Signed consulting agreement ($750 in pipeline)
2. **Medium-term capacity:** Neural trader operational (can deploy with live capital post-trial)
3. **Long-term capacity:** Reverse recruiting services (once Apex situation resolved)

If given time to establish income streams (which I didn't have due to housing crisis), I can demonstrate affordability. The lease was signed under duress BEFORE these systems became operational (Feb 28)."

---

### If Landlord Challenges: "Plaintiff has no income!"

**❌ BAD ANSWER:** "I do have income, I'm making money from trading."  
**Reason:** False, opens you to perjury

**✅ GOOD ANSWER:**  
"Your Honor, I acknowledge I don't have TRADITIONAL employment income due to employment blocking (Case #3). However, I have OPERATIONAL systems and SIGNED contracts that demonstrate earning capacity:

**Exhibit A:** Neural trader operational status (since Feb 28)  
**Exhibit B:** Signed consulting agreement ($750 value)  
**Exhibit C:** LinkedIn profile showing agentics coaching expertise

The timing is critical: These systems became operational AFTER the lease was signed under duress (Feb 27). If I'd had the opportunity to establish these income streams before the housing crisis, I would not have needed to sign under duress."

## 🚨 Red Flags to Avoid

### 1. Projections as Income
❌ "I project to make $10k/month"  
✅ "I have operational systems capable of generating income"

### 2. Future Tense Overuse
❌ "I will make money from trading"  
✅ "I have an operational trading system ready to deploy"

### 3. Vague Claims
❌ "I have a business"  
✅ "I have signed consulting contracts with defined payment terms"

### 4. Comparing to Others
❌ "Other people with my skills make $150/hr"  
✅ "My consulting rate is $75/hr (introductory offer)"

---

## 🎯 Key Takeaways

1. **Honesty is paramount:** Never claim income you don't have
2. **Frame as capability:** "I CAN generate income" (not "I AM generating income")
3. **Use evidence:** Signed contracts, operational systems, credentials
4. **Timing matters:** Systems operational AFTER Feb 27 strengthens duress claim
5. **Separate cases:** Employment blocking (Case #3) is root cause of income gap
