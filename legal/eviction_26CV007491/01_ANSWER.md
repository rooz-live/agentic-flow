# ANSWER TO COMPLAINT FOR SUMMARY EJECTMENT
## Case 26CV007491-590

---

## GOAL
Defeat landlord's summary ejectment claim, preserve tenancy, and establish counterclaims for treble damages under N.C. Gen. Stat. § 75-16 for unfair/deceptive trade practices.

---

## RESPONSE TO ALLEGATIONS

### Paragraph 1 (Parties)
**Admitted** that Plaintiff is property owner. **Denied** that Defendant is "tenant at will" – tenancy is protected under lease terms and NC Residential Rental Agreements Act.

### Paragraph 2 (Possession)
**Admitted** possession, but **Denied** unlawfully withheld. Rent tendered timely; landlord refused payment in violation of N.C. Gen. Stat. § 42-46.

### Paragraph 3 (Notice to Quit)
**Denied** valid notice served. Notice defective: (a) improper service method, (b) non-compliance with lease notice requirements, (c) retaliatory motive documented.

### Paragraph 4 (Rent Owing)
**Denied** rent unpaid. Rent deposited with Clerk of Court per G.S. § 42-34. Funds available to Plaintiff upon compliance with repair obligations.

---

## AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSES

1. **Constructive Eviction**: Landlord failed to repair HVAC (Document REPAIR_LOG_2025_01_15)
2. **Retaliatory Eviction**: Filed complaint within 60 days of repair request per G.S. § 42-37.1
3. **Breach of Warranty of Habitability**: Unit uninhabitable per inspection report (Doc INSPECT_2025_01_10)
4. **Failure to Maintain**: Violation of G.S. § 42-42(a)
5. **Unfair/Deceptive Practices**: Pattern of false rent demands, failure to credit payments

---

## COUNTERCLAIMS

### Count I: UDTPA Violation (Treble Damages)
- **Legal Basis**: N.C. Gen. Stat. § 75-16
- **Elements Met**: (1) Defendant engaged in commerce, (2) Unfair/deceptive conduct (false rent demands, ignoring repair requests), (3) Ascertainable loss (attorney fees, emotional distress, repair costs)
- **Damages Sought**: $X actual + treble + attorney fees per G.S. § 75-16.1

### Count II: Breach of Implied Warranty of Habitability
- **Damages**: Rent abatement, repair costs, alternative housing expenses

### Count III: Constructive Eviction
- **Damages**: Moving costs, deposit recovery, emotional distress
