How It Works

üé∞ Multi-Armed Bandit (Thompson Sampling)

This system uses Thompson Sampling to balance exploration (trying new content categories) and exploitation (showing categories you've liked before). It maintains a Beta distribution for each category and samples from it to decide what to recommend next. Your feedback updates the distributions in real-time, making recommendations increasingly personalized.
