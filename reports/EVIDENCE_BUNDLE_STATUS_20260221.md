## 🔍 Key Strategic Insight

### Counter-Cultural Evidence Strategy

**Traditional Legal Approach**:
- Collect EVERY piece of evidence (exhaustive)
- Prove EVERY element beyond doubt (over-documentation)
- Assume burden of proof stays with plaintiff (defensive posture)

**Strategic Approach (Applied Today)**:
- Collect **pattern evidence** that shifts burden (offensive posture)
- Use **MAA's own records** against them (portal screenshot)
- Force MAA to **defend their actions** (40+ cancellations)
- Establish **prima facie** case, let MAA struggle to rebut

**Legal Principle**: N.C.G.S. § 42-42 creates **rebuttable presumption** once tenant shows:
1. Notice of defects (✅ 40+ work orders)
2. Failure to repair (✅ cancellations/delays)
3. Materiality (✅ mold = health hazard)

**Once established, burden shifts to landlord** to prove:
- Repairs were made (MAA's records will show they weren't)
- Defects were tenant-caused (MAA must prove, difficult)
- Conditions weren't material (mold is always material)

**This is why the work order screenshot is MORE valuable than 40+ individual PDFs** — it shows the PATTERN, not just individual incidents.
