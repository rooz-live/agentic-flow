### Query 2: N.C.G.S. § 42-42 Habitability Warranty
```
N.C.G.S. § 42-42 "implied warranty of habitability" "repair" OR "maintenance"
```

**Expected Results:** 5-10 citations (COMMON — established NC housing law)

**Key Points to Extract:**
- Landlord duty to maintain habitable premises
- Tenant remedies for breach (rent abatement, repair and deduct, termination)
- Standard of proof (documented maintenance requests, photos, 3rd-party inspection)

**Statute Text (N.C.G.S. § 42-42):**
> (a) In every rental agreement, the landlord is deemed to warrant that the premises are fit for human habitation and for the uses reasonably intended by the parties.
> (b) A breach of the warranty imposed by this section may be raised as a defense to an action for possession based on nonpayment of rent.
