<div class="section">
<h2>⚖️ LEGAL AMMUNITION</h2>

<div class="box">
<h3>1. Von Pettis Realty v. McKoy (NC precedent)</h3>
<p><strong>Holding:</strong> When a residential unit is uninhabitable, tenant owes <span class="highlight">ZERO rent</span>.</p>
<p><strong>Your Use:</strong> "Your Honor, Von Pettis is clear — 22 months of mold, HVAC failures, and electrical hazards = uninhabitable unit = zero rent owed."</p>
</div>

<div class="box">
<h3>2. N.C.G.S. § 42-42 (Implied Warranty of Habitability)</h3>
<p><strong>Standard:</strong> Landlord must maintain fit premises for habitation.</p>
<p><strong>Your Use:</strong> "40+ work orders over 22 months = systemic breach of implied warranty."</p>
</div>

<div class="box">
<h3>3. N.C.G.S. § 42-37.1 (Retaliatory Eviction)</h3>
<p><strong>Standard:</strong> Eviction within 12 months of habitability complaint = rebuttable presumption of retaliation.</p>
<p><strong>Your Use:</strong> "Eviction filed Feb 9, 2026 during settlement talks = textbook retaliation."</p>
</div>

<div class="box">
<h3>4. N.C.G.S. § 1D-15 (Punitive Damages)</h3>
<p><strong>Standard:</strong> 3x compensatory for willful/wanton conduct.</p>
<p><strong>Your Use:</strong> "Systemically canceling 40+ work orders over 22 months = willful neglect justifying 3x multiplier."</p>
</div>
</div>
