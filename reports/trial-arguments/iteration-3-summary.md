## KEY PHRASE REHEARSAL BLOCKS

### BLOCK 1: INCOME CAPACITY FRAME
**Primary Phrase:** *"Income capacity versus current income state"*
**Timing Target:** 45-60 seconds

**Rehearsal Script:**
*"Your Honor, the distinction is critical. **Income capacity** - my ability to generate revenue through established protocols and partnerships - remained intact even when **current income** was blocked by the restrictive covenant. The evidence shows [PAUSE] $47,000 in consulting pipeline, documented client relationships, and proven methodologies. This wasn't **zero income** - this was **blocked income**."*

**Pivot Preparation:**
- If interrupted: *"Respectfully, Your Honor, may I clarify the income distinction?"*
- If challenged: *"To clarify - capacity differs from current state..."*

### BLOCK 2: DURESS TIMING PRECISION  
**Primary Phrase:** *"Couldn't then, can now"*
**Timing Target:** 60-75 seconds

**Rehearsal Script:**
*"Your Honor, the temporal frame is essential. December 2023 - I **couldn't** access my income streams due to the restrictive covenant's immediate effect. **Then** - financial pressure, limited options, signed under duress. **Now** - with distance and evidence, we can see the true picture. The $200K+ income potential was always there, just artificially blocked."*

**Anti-Compatible Tension Practice:**
- *"The capacity existed THEN, but access was blocked THEN"*
- *"I can demonstrate NOW what was always possible THEN"*

### BLOCK 3: EMPLOYMENT BLOCKING MECHANISM
**Primary Phrase:** *"Systematic employment interference"*  
**Timing Target:** 75-90 seconds

**Rehearsal Script:**
*"Your Honor, this wasn't passive restriction. The evidence shows **systematic blocking**: direct contact with potential employers, threatening legal action against consulting clients, creating a 'legal risk' perception that dried up opportunities. When someone can't earn, they become vulnerable to signing anything that promises relief."*

### BLOCK 4: HABITABILITY STANDARD
**Primary Phrase:** *"Habitability includes economic habitability"*
**Timing Target:** 60-80 seconds

**Rehearsal Script:**
*"Your Honor, habitability isn't just physical - it's **economic habitability**. When living conditions deteriorate because income is artificially blocked, that creates the same duress as physical uninhabitable conditions. The law recognizes economic duress as grounds for contract voidability."*

## COUNTER-ARGUMENT RESPONSE REHEARSAL (#36-42)

### COUNTER #1: "You signed willingly"
**Response Framework (60s max):**
*"Your Honor, **willingness** under duress isn't true consent. The evidence shows I signed because my income was blocked, creating artificial urgency. **As I was saying earlier** - capacity existed, but access was systematically denied. That's not willing - that's coercive."*

### COUNTER #2: "Zero income means zero capacity"  
**Response Framework (75s max):**
*"**To clarify**, Your Honor - zero **current** income doesn't equal zero **capacity**. The consulting pipeline evidence shows $47,000 in blocked opportunities. **Respectfully**, the distinction matters legally - duress comes from blocked potential, not absent ability."*

### COUNTER #3: "Paper trading isn't real income"
**Response Framework (80s max):**
*"Your Honor, paper trading was **preparation** for real trading, not the income source itself. The real income came from consulting, partnerships, and methodologies - all documented and blocked. **As I was explaining** - multiple revenue streams were systematically interfered with."*

### COUNTER #4: "Self-serving contract interpretation"
**Response Framework (85s max):**
*"**Respectfully, Your Honor**, interpretation follows evidence. The timeline shows: income blocked → pressure mounted → contract signed → relief promised but not delivered. This isn't self-serving - this is the documented sequence that courts recognize as classic duress patterns."*
